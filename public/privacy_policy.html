Last updated: February 18, 2026

"nQws" is a product of Effedue Studio. We believe in privacy by design.

Effedue Studio ("we," "our," or "us") operates the nQws mobile application (the "Service"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service and the choices you have associated with that data.

1. Information Collection

Personal Data

We are committed to user privacy. We do not require you to create an account, sign up, or provide any personally identifiable information (such as your name, email address, or phone number) to use the Service.

Usage Data

When you access the Service, standard usage logs may be generated by our server infrastructure. This may include your device's Internet Protocol (IP) address, device type, and the time and date of your requests. This data is used solely for diagnostic and security purposes.

Local Data Storage

To provide a seamless experience, nQws stores specific data locally on your device:

  • User Preferences: Settings such as theme choice (Light/Dark), font size, and language preference.
  • Read History: A local list of articles you have opened, used to visually indicate which news items you have already read.

This data resides solely on your device and is not synchronized to any external server by us.

2. Use of Data

Effedue Studio uses the collected data for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain the Service.
  • To monitor usage and detect technical issues.
  • To improve the performance and user experience of the application.

3. Third-Party Services

We may employ third-party companies to facilitate our Service. These third parties have access to your personal data only to perform these tasks on our behalf and are obligated not to disclose or use it for any other purpose.

Cloudflare

We use Cloudflare to host our API endpoints. Cloudflare acts as a security and performance layer. By using the app, your requests pass through Cloudflare's network.

Local Data: Data preferences and history stored on your device are retained until you manually clear the app data or uninstall the application.
Server Logs: Usage data collected by our infrastructure providers is retained for a limited period as per their standard security and logging policies.

The security of your data is important to us. We use commercially reasonable means to protect your data, but please remember that no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

6. Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal data associated with a user account, you act as the controller of your local data. You can exercise your rights by:

  • Access & Deletion: You can view and delete your locally stored data at any time by clearing the application's storage in your device settings or by uninstalling the app.
  • Opt-Out: You can stop all collection of information by the Application easily by uninstalling the Application.

7. Children's Privacy

Our Service does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us.
